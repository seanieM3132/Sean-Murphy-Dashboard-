## Workout Routines

### Workout A
- Wide pushups: 4 sets x 10
- Normal pushups: 4 sets x 10
- Burpees (pushup → squat → max jump): 5 sets x 10
- Squats: 5 sets x 10
- Knees to Chest: 3 sets x 15

### Workout B
- Walking pushups: Pushup → walk hands 90° right → pushup → walk hands left → pushup → back to start → pushup = 1 rep. Men: 4 sets x 7 (add 5th set if able). Women: 4 sets x 4
- Lunges: 5 sets x 10 each leg (knee behind toes)
- Add a set where needed if too simple

### Workout C
- Clap push-ups: Men 5 sets x 15 (or max), Women 3 sets x 10 (or max)
- Calf jumps in 1/4 squat: bounce on toes, 5 sets x 30
- Calf jumps: jump straight up max height, 5 sets x 15

### Workout D
- Decline push-ups (feet on chair): 5 sets x 10
- Diamond push-ups: Men 5 sets x 11, Women 4 sets x 7
- Vertical wall jumps (slap wall as high as possible): 5 sets x 20
- Single leg jumps (jump and land same leg): 5 sets x 10 each leg
- Calf raises (hold 5 sec): Men 10 sets x 20, Women 15 sets x 10

## Core Routines

### Abs 1
1. 25 slow crunches
2. 25 full sit-ups (careful with back)
3. 25 reps — 6 inch scissor (legs 6" off ground, cross legs twice = 1 rep)
4. 3 sets — Superman hold (stomach down, lift shoulders and legs, hold 5 sec)
5. 25 slow crunches

### Abs 2
- 3 sets x 1 min each (add sets as it gets easy):
  1. Front plank
  2. Left side plank
  3. Right side plank

## Jump Rope Routines

### Jump Rope 1
- 5 sets x 30 jumps, 30 sec rest between sets

### Jump Rope 2
- 3 sets x 100 jumps, 30 sec rest between sets

*(No rope? Do the movements without one — still get aerobic and plyometric benefits)*
