# Ball Work Sessions — Full Drill Breakdown
> Every drill has sets, reps/duration, rest, foot, and coaching cues.
> Touch the ball EVERY day. Minimum 15 min, ideal 30-45 min.
> Sean = CM (8/6), right-foot dominant, left foot very strong.

---

## Session Structure (30-45 min)

| Block | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| 1. Warm-Up Touches | 5 min | Get comfortable on the ball |
| 2. Ball Mastery | 10 min | Close control, speed of feet |
| 3. First Touch & Receiving | 5-8 min | CM-specific: receiving under pressure |
| 4. Passing & Switching | 5-8 min | Short/long range, both feet |
| 5. Weak Foot Block | 5 min | Left foot only (or right if alternating) |
| 6. Long Throw-In Practice | 5-10 min | 2-3x/week (Mon, Wed, Fri) |

## 1. Warm-Up Touches (5 min)

| Drill | Duration | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|----------|------|------|------|
| Toe taps on ball | 30 sec | Both (alternating) | 0 | Light and quick, stay on balls of feet |
| Inside-inside rolls | 30 sec | Both | 0 | Roll ball side to side between inside of feet, low center of gravity |
| Sole rolls forward/back | 30 sec R, 30 sec L | Each | 0 | Pull ball back with sole, push forward with laces |
| Foundation (ball between feet) | 30 sec | Both | 0 | Inside R → inside L, keep ball tight, head up |
| Freestyle juggling | 2 min | Both | 0 | Mix feet, thighs, head. Goal: 50+ touches without drop |

## 2. Ball Mastery Drills (10 min)

### Set A — Joner Football Style (Weeks 1-2)

| Drill | Sets x Duration | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|----------------|------|------|------|
| Sole drag → inside push | 4 x 20 sec | R then L (2 each) | 10 sec | Drag back with sole, push forward with inside. SPEED |
| Inside-outside cut | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Inside touch across body, outside touch to push away. Sharp cuts |
| Iniesta (sole drag behind standing leg) | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Drag behind, pop out the other side. Smooth, head UP |
| V-drag | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Pull back with sole, push 45° with inside. Explosive change |
| La Croqueta (inside-inside) | 4 x 20 sec | Both | 10 sec | Inside R → inside L in quick succession. Tight space skill |

### Set B — Takuya Sole Skills (Weeks 3-4, rotate)

| Drill | Sets x Duration | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|----------------|------|------|------|
| Sole roll stepover | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Roll across body with sole, stepover, push opposite direction |
| Fake shot → sole pull | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Wind up like shooting, pull ball back with sole instead |
| Matthews cut (outside hook) | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Push ball outside, sharp cut inside with same foot |
| Elastico | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Outside push → inside flick in one motion. Whip the ankle |
| Berbatov wall drill (if wall available) | 4 x 30 sec | Both | 15 sec | Pass to wall, first touch control, pass again. Rhythm and tempo |

### Set C — Cognitive Ball Mastery (Weeks 5+, rotate)

| Drill | Sets x Duration | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|----------------|------|------|------|
| Sole drags + tennis ball catch | 4 x 30 sec | Both | 15 sec | Partner tosses tennis ball while you do sole drags. EYES OFF THE BALL |
| Coerver Pull-Push speed test | 4 x 20 sec | R then L | 10 sec | Pull back → push forward between 2 cones. Count reps, beat your number |
| Ball mastery + color call | 4 x 30 sec | Both | 15 sec | Partner holds up colored cones, you call color while doing mastery |
| Freestyle combo chains | 4 x 30 sec | Both | 15 sec | Chain 3 skills together without stopping. Create flow |

## 3. First Touch & Receiving — CM Specific (5-8 min)

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|------|------|------|
| Wall pass → open body first touch | 3 x 10 | R, then 3 x 10 L | 15 sec between sets | Pass to wall, open hips, receive on half-turn. Head up BEFORE the ball arrives |
| Wall pass → back foot reception | 3 x 10 | R, then 3 x 10 L | 15 sec | Let ball come across body, receive on back foot. Creates space away from pressure |
| Chest control → volley pass | 3 x 8 | Both | 15 sec | Throw ball up, chest down, volley to target on first or second touch |
| Ground receive → turn & play | 3 x 8 each side | Both | 15 sec | Receive with inside, body already turning, play forward. One fluid motion |
| Lofted ball → thigh/chest → set → pass | 3 x 6 | Both | 20 sec | Throw ball high, control on thigh or chest, set with foot, pass to wall/target |

**CM mindset:** Always know where you're playing BEFORE the ball arrives. Scan — shoulder check — receive — play. Never receive blind.

## 4. Passing & Switching Play (5-8 min)

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|------|------|------|
| Short pass accuracy (5m target) | 3 x 10 | R, then 3 x 10 L | 10 sec | Hit a cone or mark from 5m. Inside of foot. Aim for the base |
| Driven pass (15-20m to wall/target) | 3 x 8 | R, then 3 x 8 L | 15 sec | Lock ankle, strike through center of ball, keep it on the ground. FIRM |
| Lofted switch (25-30m) | 3 x 6 | R, then 3 x 6 L | 20 sec | Open hips, lean back slightly, strike under ball with laces. Land on a dime |
| One-two off wall (quick combo) | 4 x 30 sec | Both | 15 sec | Pass → move → receive → pass. Simulate give-and-go. Speed of play |
| Ping long ball (7mlc technique) | 3 x 8 | R, then 3 x 8 L | 20 sec | Plant foot beside ball, lock ankle, lean back, strike through lower half of ball with laces. Follow through to target |

## 5. Weak Foot Block (5 min)

**Rule:** If right-foot dominant, this block is LEFT foot only. Alternate weekly if both feet are strong.

| Drill | Duration | Foot | Rest | Cues |
|-------|----------|------|------|------|
| Inside-outside rolls | 1 min | L only | 0 | Smooth, controlled. Don't rush |
| Wall passing (inside foot) | 2 min | L only | 0 | 5m from wall. Clean strike, clean reception. Build rhythm |
| Sole drag → inside push | 1 min | L only | 0 | Same as mastery drill but L foot only |
| Driven pass to target | 1 min | L only | 0 | 15m to target. Lock ankle, firm strike. Every rep counts |

## 6. Long Throw-In Training (5-10 min, Mon/Wed/Fri)

> Source: Long Throw Coach online course. Target: Gain distance over 2-4 weeks.

### Warm-Up (2 min)
| Exercise | Reps | Rest | Cues |
|----------|------|------|------|
| Arm circles (forward) | 10 each arm | 0 | Full range, loosen shoulders |
| Arm circles (backward) | 10 each arm | 0 | Controlled |
| Overhead stretch + side bend | 15 sec each side | 0 | Open up lats and obliques |
| Light throw-ins (50% effort) | 5 throws | 10 sec between | Groove the movement pattern |

### Technique Drills (5-8 min)
| Drill | Sets x Reps | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|------|------|
| Standing long throw (feet together) | 3 x 5 | 30 sec | Both feet planted. Focus: ball starts behind head, arch the back, SNAP through with core + lats. Both hands equal force. Follow through with both arms to target |
| One-step approach throw | 3 x 5 | 30 sec | One step forward momentum. Plant front foot hard. Transfer weight forward. Use the ground reaction force |
| Run-up long throw (3-5 steps) | 3 x 5 | 45 sec | Short run-up, plant, lean back, explode forward. Ball must come from BEHIND the head — ref will call foul throw otherwise |
| Max distance attempts | 5 x 1 | 60 sec | Full effort. Measure distance with landmarks. Track progress weekly |

### Key Coaching Points
- Ball must start behind the head and travel over the head (Law 15)
- Both feet must stay on or behind the touchline
- Core and lat power matter more than arms — it's a full-body whip
- Arch the back like a bow, snap forward like releasing an arrow
- Grip: fingers spread wide, thumbs behind the ball, pinkies almost touching underneath
- Target: 25m+ to be a weapon at college level
