### **I. Fascia Training & Routines**

Fascia is a continuous web of connective tissue that holds the body together through **tensegrity** (tension + integrity) [19].

*   **Remodeling Protocol:** Basic fascia exercises should be performed **three times a week** to allow for slow adaptation and tissue remodeling [20].
*   **Standard Programming:** A typical fascia-focused program spans **12 weeks** [20].
*   **Foot-to-Glute Connection:** Training begins at the feet to create upstream neural and structural connections to the glutes [19].
*   **Elevated Toe Curls:** These can be performed daily as a foundational beginner exercise [20].

### **II. Injury Prevention Protocols (ACL & General)**

#### **1. PEP Program (ACL Prevention)**
This 15-20 minute routine can reduce ACL injuries by up to 70% and must be done in the specific order below [33].

*   **Avoidance (Warm-up):**
    *   **Light Jog:** 1 minute (3-4 times) [34].
    *   **Side-to-Side Shuttle:** 1 minute [34].
    *   **Backward Run:** 1 minute [35].
*   **Flexibility (30 sec each side):** Calf stretch (V-formation), quad stretch (standing), figure-4 hamstring stretch, inner thigh stretch, and hip flexor stretch [35, 36].
*   **Strength:**
    *   **Walking Lunges:** 1 minute [36].
    *   **Single-Leg Bridge:** 30 sec per side [37].
    *   **Single Toe Raise:** 30 sec per side [38].
*   **Plyometrics:**
    *   **Side-to-Side Hops (over cone):** 20 reps (30 sec) [38].
    *   **Front-Back Hops:** 30 sec [38].
    *   **Single-Leg Hops:** 30 sec per side [39].
    *   **Scissor Jumps:** 30 sec [39].
    *   **Coaching Cue:** **Land softly on the balls of your feet** with knees slightly bent and hips straight to engage hamstrings and protect the ACL [38].

#### **2. FIFA 11+ Running Exercises**
Perform **two sets** of each exercise:
*   Jog straight (maintain hip/knee/foot alignment) [40].
*   Hip Out / Hip In (rotate knee while jogging) [40].
*   Circling partner / Shoulder contact (land with hips/knees bent) [40].
*   Quick forward and backward runs [40].

### **III. Mobility & Recovery Protocols**

*   **Diaphragmatic Breathing:** Inhale until the belly extends to create a pressure differential that clears the **cisterna chyli** (lymph reservoir) [41, 42]. Perform several breaths throughout the day to reduce swelling and improve energy [43, 44].
*   **Glymphatic Clearance (Brain Waste):** To reduce brain fog and facial puffiness, **sleep on your side** with the head slightly tilted up via a pillow [45-47].
*   **Lymphatic Drainage:** Use "low-level muscular contractions" such as **walking (7,000+ steps)**, swimming, or rebounding on a small trampoline to move lymph fluid without a pump [48-50].
*   **Fascial Face Massage:** Use light, order-specific pressure on the neck and clavicle region to encourage drainage and improve facial appearance [51, 52].
