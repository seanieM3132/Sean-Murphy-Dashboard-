## Morning Protocol (EVERY DAY — Non-Negotiable)

| Time | Activity | Why / Source |
|------|----------|--------------|
| 7:00 | Wake. NO SNOOZE. No phone for 15 min | Avoid reactive dopamine spike (Huberman) |
| 7:00 | Open blinds / step outside immediately | Sunlight in eyes within 30 min of waking — 10 min minimum. Triggers 50% cortisol pulse increase, clears adenosine, sets melatonin timer for night (Huberman) |
| 7:10 | Hydrate: 500ml water + pinch salt + electrolytes | Rehydrate from overnight. Electrolytes in morning water (Galpin protocol) |
| 7:15 | 10-min optic flow walk (outside, sunlight) | Forward ambulation + panoramic vision reduces amygdala activation, increases calm alertness (Huberman) |
| 7:25 | **EAT BREAKFAST** — never skip | 3 eggs + toast + fruit OR oats + banana + protein shake. 30-60g carbs + 20g protein minimum |
| 7:50 | Creatine 5g (with breakfast) | Daily, no skipping. Consistency is everything |
| 8:00 | Begin training block | See day-specific schedule below |

**Caffeine rule:** Delay caffeine 90-120 min after waking (until ~8:30-9:00am) to let natural cortisol clear adenosine first. Exception: match day — take 30-50 min before kickoff. Dose: 1-3mg/kg bodyweight.

## Evening Protocol (EVERY DAY — Non-Negotiable)

| Time | Activity | Why / Source |
|------|----------|--------------|
| 8:00 | Begin wind-down. Reduce overhead lights, use dim/warm lighting | Bright light 10pm-4am suppresses dopamine by 50% for days (Huberman) |
| 8:30 | No intense screen content. Read, visualize, watch calm game film | Reduce cortisol/adrenaline before sleep |
| 9:00 | Sauna 15-20 min at 80-100°C (if not done earlier) | Compensatory cooling response 1-2hrs later helps you fall asleep. Core temp must drop 1-3°F to sleep deeply (Huberman) |
| 9:30 | Sleep supplement stack | Magnesium Threonate 145mg + Apigenin 50mg + L-Theanine 100-400mg (Huberman stack) |

**If you can't sleep within 20 min:** Do a 10-min NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest) protocol — YouTube "Huberman NSDR". Don't lie there stressing.

## GOATA Groundwork (Add to EVERY Warm-Up)

Do these BEFORE any training session. 45-60 seconds each. Total: ~5 min.

| Exercise | Duration | Cues |
|----------|----------|------|
| Ground Hip Stretch | 45-60 sec each side | All fours, cross one leg over, keep Inside Ankle Bone High (IABH), head up |
| Child Rocker | 60 sec x 3 sets | Toes together, heels out, head up, as butt goes down ankle bone climbs higher. 15 sec rest between sets |
| Toe Tuck Rocker | 60 sec x 3 sets | Same as child rocker but toes tucked, heels open during the rock. 15 sec rest |

**GOATA principles to carry into ALL movement:** Inside Ankle Bone High, Back Chain Dominance, Column Alignment, Heel Away from midline on ground contact.
