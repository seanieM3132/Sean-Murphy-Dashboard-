# Gym Program — Full Programming with Weights
> Progressive overload system. Starting weights are estimates based on Sean's profile.
> Sean: 19yo, ~5'10, medium/athletic build, inconsistent training history, no injuries.
> Adjust starting weights on Day 1 — if it's too easy, go heavier. If form breaks, drop it.

---

## Progressive Overload Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Rep range** | Every exercise has a target range (e.g., 3x6-8) |
| **When to increase** | When you complete ALL sets at the TOP of the rep range with clean form → increase weight next session |
| **Upper body increase** | +2.5kg per session |
| **Lower body increase** | +5kg per session |
| **If you fail** | If you can't hit the BOTTOM of the rep range → drop weight 10% and rebuild |
| **Tempo** | Unless stated otherwise: 2 sec down, 1 sec pause, 1 sec up (2-1-1) |
| **Rest between sets** | Compounds: 2-3 min. Accessories: 60-90 sec. Core: 30-60 sec |
| **Log everything** | Weight, reps, sets. Every session. No guessing |

## Monday — Heavy Compound Day (Seward + Gym)

Do after Seward workout if Seward is bodyweight-focused. If Seward is sprints/running, do gym in the afternoon.

### Gym Warm-Up (Before Main Lifts — 5 min)

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Cues |
|----------|------------|------|
| Empty bar squat | 2 x 8 | Groove the pattern, full depth |
| Empty bar RDL | 1 x 8 | Feel the hamstring stretch |
| Ramp-up set 1 | 1 x 5 @ 50% working weight | Building up |
| Ramp-up set 2 | 1 x 3 @ 75% working weight | Nearly there |
| Then start working sets | | |

### Main Lifts

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Starting Weight | Rest | Tempo | Cues |
|----------|------------|----------------|------|-------|------|
| Back Squat | 4 x 6-8 | 50kg | 2-3 min | 3-1-1 | Feet shoulder width, break at hips first, knees track toes, chest up, hit parallel minimum. Brace core hard |
| Romanian Deadlift (RDL) | 4 x 6-8 | 45kg | 2-3 min | 3-1-1 | Slight knee bend, hinge at hips, bar slides down thighs, feel hamstring stretch, squeeze glutes to stand. FLAT BACK |
| Barbell Bench Press | 3 x 6-8 | 45kg | 2 min | 2-1-1 | Retract shoulder blades, feet flat on floor, bar touches mid-chest, drive up explosively |
| Barbell Row | 3 x 8-10 | 40kg | 90 sec | 2-1-1 | Hinge 45°, pull to lower chest, squeeze shoulder blades, no momentum/swinging |

### Accessories

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Starting Weight | Rest | Cues |
|----------|------------|----------------|------|------|
| Pull-ups | 3 x max (aim 5-8) | Bodyweight | 90 sec | Full dead hang to chin over bar. If can't do 5, use band assist |
| Dumbbell Shoulder Press | 3 x 8-10 | 14kg each | 90 sec | Seated or standing. Full lockout overhead. Control the descent |
| Face Pulls (cable or band) | 3 x 15 | Light band | 60 sec | Pull to face height, external rotate at end. Shoulder health |

### Core (Stiffness Focus — Monday)

| Exercise | Sets x Duration/Reps | Rest | Cues |
|----------|---------------------|------|------|
| Front Plank | 3 x 45 sec | 30 sec | Elbows under shoulders, squeeze glutes, no sagging. If easy, add 10kg plate on back |
| Pallof Press | 3 x 10 each side | 30 sec | Band or cable at chest height. Press out, hold 2 sec, resist rotation. Brace |
| Dead Bugs | 3 x 8 each side | 30 sec | Lower back GLUED to floor. Opposite arm/leg extend. Slow and controlled |

### Plyometrics (BEFORE main lifts, after warm-up, when fresh)

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Rest | Cues |
|----------|------------|------|------|
| Box Jumps (50-60cm) | 3 x 5 | 60 sec | Explode up, land soft with knees bent. Step down, don't jump down |
| Broad Jumps | 3 x 5 | 60 sec | Swing arms, explode forward, stick the landing. Measure distance |
| Single-Leg Bounds | 3 x 5 each leg | 45 sec | Drive knee up, cover ground, land on same leg. Power and balance |

### Main Lifts

|----------|------------|----------------|------|-------|------|

## Speed & Acceleration Sessions (Outside Seward)

> Sean's #1 weakness. Add 1x/week dedicated speed session when no Seward sprint day scheduled.

### Sprint Mechanics Warm-Up (5 min)

| Drill | Reps | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------|------|------|
| A-skips | 2 x 20m | Walk back | Drive knee up, pop off ground. Posture tall |
| B-skips | 2 x 20m | Walk back | Knee up → extend and claw ground backward |
| High knees | 2 x 20m | Walk back | Fast turnover, stay tall, arms driving |
| Wall drives | 2 x 8 each leg | 15 sec | Hands on wall 45°, drive knee up, switch. POWER |
| Falling starts | 3 x 15m | Walk back | Lean forward until you fall, EXPLODE into sprint |

### Acceleration Block (First 10m — the most important for soccer)

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Distance | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|----------|------|------|
| Standing start sprints | 5 x 1 | 10m | 60 sec | Low start, 45° body angle, short choppy steps, drive into ground BEHIND you. First 3 steps are everything |
| 3-point start sprints | 4 x 1 | 15m | 60 sec | Hand on ground, explode out. Gradually rise to upright by 10m |
| Reaction sprints (partner clap/call) | 4 x 1 | 10m | 60 sec | Wait for signal, EXPLODE. Train reaction speed + first step |

### Top Speed Block

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Distance | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|----------|------|------|
| Flying 20s (10m build → 20m max) | 4 x 1 | 30m total | 90 sec | Build to 80% in first 10m, hit MAX for the next 20m. Upright posture, knees high, relax face and hands |
| Tempo runs | 3 x 1 | 100m | 60 sec | 70-75% effort. Build aerobic base for speed endurance. Smooth and relaxed |

### Soccer-Specific Speed

| Drill | Sets x Reps | Rest | Cues |
|-------|------------|------|------|
| 5-10-5 shuttle | 4 x 1 | 90 sec | Sprint 5m, touch, sprint 10m opposite way, touch, sprint 5m back. LOW on the turns |
| T-drill | 3 x 1 | 90 sec | Sprint forward 10m, shuffle right 5m, shuffle left 10m, shuffle right 5m, backpedal 10m. Speed + agility |
| Ball retrieval sprint | 4 x 1 | 60 sec | Ball placed 15m away. Sprint to it, control with first touch, dribble back. Game-realistic |
