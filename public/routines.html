Caffeine Rule

Delay caffeine 90-120 min after waking (until ~8:30-9:00am) to let natural cortisol clear adenosine first. Exception: match day — take 30-50 min before kickoff. Dose: 1-3mg/kg.

  • 8:00
    Begin wind-down. Reduce overhead lights, use dim/warm lighting
    Bright light 10pm-4am suppresses dopamine by 50% for days (Huberman)
  • 8:30
    No intense screen content. Read, visualize, watch calm game film
    Reduce cortisol/adrenaline before sleep
  • 9:00
    Sauna 15-20 min at 80-100C (if scheduled)
    Compensatory cooling 1-2hrs later helps fall asleep. Core temp must drop 1-3F (Huberman)
  • 9:30
    Sleep supplement stack
    Magnesium Threonate 145mg + Apigenin 50mg + L-Theanine 100-400mg
  • 9:45

    Side sleeping maximizes glymphatic clearance (brain waste removal). Nose = better O2 exchange
  • 10:00
